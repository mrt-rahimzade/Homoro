About Me

My name is Morteza Rahimzadeh. I'm an illustrator and motion designer with a strong interest in combining mathematics and technical knowledge with clear, practical visual aesthetics. I enjoy creating work where structured thinking supports simple, effective visuals.

I focus on producing content that brings sound, text, and imagery together in motion, with careful attention to details, timing, and project management. My goal is to deliver clean, reliable results that meet the needs of each brand or project.

If you're curious about creating visual content for your brand or business, feel free to contact me. I'm always open to discussing ideas and planning the best approach for your project.
